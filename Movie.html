My Stupid Boss

This movie is a fictional story about a crazy and a stupid boss called “Bossman”. One day there is a girl looking for a job name “Diana” she also has a husband name “Dika”. Her husband is a friend of Bossman, the guy that owns a car service company, then Dika told Diana to apply for a job in Dika friend company. She was accepted and has his first day of work. In first day of work Diana was kind of confuse because the boss was Joking too much that makes her annoyed because the Bossman called Diana as “Kera” or an monkey. One day at work Diana were invited to a restaurant with Bossman, at the restaurant Bossman saw a business man and have the guy eating together with Bossman and Diana, Bossman said that he will pay for the food but after that Bossman was lying to go to the bank but the Bossman was actually run away because he can’t paid the bills because it was expensive. The Bossman also forgot that he left Diana at the restaurant. After it happens Diana was so mad at the Boss that makes she want to leave the work.

Mencuri Raden Saleh

This movie is a fictional story about a story of a group of young people who try to steal a painting called “Penangkapan Pangeran Diponegoro”, this film has successfully won several best film awards. At the 2022 Indonesian Film Festival, Mencuri Raden Saleh was included in nine nominations including Film with Best Long Story. The biggest heist of the century is just days away from his execution date. The gang is complete and ready to carry out the mission to steal the painting by the maestro, the gang consist of Piko as the guy who recreate the replication of Raden Saleh painting, Tuktuk as the professional driver, Fella as the rich women who can buy anything, Gofar as the second driver, Yusuf Hamdan as the hacker, Permadi is the enemy, and a lot more character. They wanted to steal the real Raden Saleh painting entitled Arrest of Diponegoro. Counterfeiting, hacking, fighting and manipulation ensue in a heist plot by a group of young amateurs.

Orang Kaya Baru

This movie is a fictional story about a poor family that consist of Lukman Sadri or known as the father, Cut Mini known as the mother, Tika as the first child, Duta as the second child, and Dodi as the youngest child. The family wishes to be rich someday because they can’t live happily or getting bullied by friends until one day their father died. Since their father died, father leave a message to a bank before he died, father said that he has a lot of money saved to his bank account that could make the family rich, the family was really happy and started to bought a big house, an expensive car, and trying to waste all of the money. One day things were getting turned, because they were ran out of money because they spend it all, Then everyone started to get upset and worried because they have so many stuff that they could go poor again, until they really go poor again. But they were suddenly happy to be poor again because it was better than being rich.
