Aku Pasti Kembali - Pasto

This song is written by Maia Estianty, this song is based on a true story that Maia (the writer) had experienced in her life. So Maia mother had to go for a work, while Maia and her family crying, sad that her mother need to leave her family for 7 years and needs to take a long journey just for a work. Then to make their kids feeling better, Maia had an idea to create a song about it, “Aku pasti akan Kembali” means Maia mother will be coming back and she just need to go for a while. Maia started to write the song lyrics then she collaborate with the group band name “Grup Ratu”, after this song was made it was a big surprise, Maia brother was feeling so much better and started to sing together, the song was really exciting and good that makes another band name “Pasto” tried to cover the song with acoustic genre, the group band “Pasto” make the song so much better that makes the song go viral and was heard in the radio for many times.

Kangen - Dewa 19

In 1991 a group band name “Dewa 19” is struggling to get record contract. Since then “Dewa 19” is not profitable for their company records that makes them struggle. After they tried to go everywhere and asking for a record contract they finally got a record contract at “Kla Project”. Dewa 19 started to make a lot of song, the group band members is really professional at making music that makes the song starting to go viral back then, Dewa 19 makes the team records shocks that the song go that viral. Then Dewa 19 moved to a bigger company record called “Aquarius muskindo”. Because of that beautiful story, Dewa 19 make a song title “Kangen” that inspirated from a group band called “White Lion” song “You’re All I Need” they make the song inspirated from White Lion because they like White Lion so much.
