Penangkapan Pangeran Diponegoro

Penangkapan Pangeran Diponegoro is a painting by Raden Saleh that is made in March 28, 1830 which depicts the capture of Prince Diponegoro by Lieutenant General Hendrik Merkus De Kock. This painting is to take a revenge of Netherland, because Netherland painters ever make an art painting showing that Netherland having a victory over Indonesia. Then Raden Saleh had an idea to turn back the table by making this painting showing that Indonesia showing a victory showing the Indonesia flag is up and the Netherland army prostate to Indonesia army.
